### Bagian 16 — FAQ

#### Apakah IhsanCloud benar-benar gratis?

Fitur inti perangkat lunak tersedia secara gratis bagi lembaga yang memenuhi ketentuan program. Implementasi, migrasi data, pelatihan, kustomisasi, integrasi, dan dukungan khusus dapat dikenakan biaya.

#### Mengapa IhsanCloud membutuhkan donasi?

Walaupun perangkat lunaknya gratis, layanan tetap membutuhkan server, database, penyimpanan, keamanan, pemeliharaan, pengembangan, pelatihan, dan dukungan pengguna.

#### Untuk apa donasi digunakan?

Donasi digunakan untuk operasional infrastruktur, pemeliharaan sistem, pengembangan produk, pelatihan, dokumentasi, pendampingan, dan pengembangan jaringan mitra.

#### Siapa yang mendapatkan manfaat?

Sekolah, ma’had, TPQ, masjid, yayasan, lembaga dakwah, pengajar, peserta didik, orang tua, jamaah, dan masyarakat.

#### Apakah donasi bersifat wajib bagi lembaga pengguna?

Tidak. Donasi merupakan dukungan sukarela. Lembaga dapat menggunakan fitur inti sesuai ketentuan program tanpa diwajibkan menjadi donatur.

#### Apakah data antar-lembaga terpisah?

Ya. Setiap lembaga memiliki ruang kerja dan hak akses masing-masing sesuai arsitektur multi-tenant.

#### Apakah tersedia pelatihan?

Tersedia panduan mandiri, pelatihan operator, dan program pendampingan sesuai kebutuhan.

#### Apakah lembaga dapat meminta fitur khusus?

Kebutuhan dapat diajukan untuk dipertimbangkan dalam roadmap atau dikembangkan melalui layanan khusus.

#### Apakah donatur akan menerima laporan?

Laporan penggunaan donasi dan perkembangan layanan akan disampaikan secara berkala.

## 18. Prompt Utama untuk Coding Agent

```text
Build a production-ready landing page for IhsanCloud using Astro,
TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, the installed Hallmark skill, and Vercel.

IhsanCloud is a free core software ecosystem for Islamic schools,
ma'had, pesantren, TPQ, mosques, foundations, jamaah communities,
and da'wah institutions.

The product has two equally important conversion paths:

1. Institutions can register to use the core software for free.
2. Individuals and organizations can become recurring donors to keep
   the platform live, secure, maintained, developed, and accessible.

Core positioning:
- Free core software for eligible institutions.
- No mandatory software license fee.
- Sustainability is supported through recurring operational donations,
  sponsorship, implementation services, training, and partnerships.
- Donations support servers, database, storage, backups, security,
  maintenance, product development, documentation, operator training,
  institution onboarding, technical support, and partner outreach.
- The impact reaches institution leaders, teachers, staff, thousands
  of students and santri, parents, guardians, jamaah, and communities.

Primary audience:
- school principals
- ma'had directors
- TPQ administrators
- DKM and mosque administrators
- foundation leaders
- teachers and operators
- donors
- sponsors
- implementation partners

Primary CTAs:
- Daftarkan Lembaga
- Jadi Donatur Tetap

Design direction:
- calm institutional technology
- trustworthy, warm, editorial, spacious
- warm ivory background
- deep olive primary
- forest green secondary
- charcoal text
- restrained muted gold accents
- no generic purple or blue SaaS gradients
- no repetitive equal-sized feature card grids
- no human or animal illustrations
- no mosque arches, lanterns, crescents, decorative calligraphy,
  or irrelevant Islamic ornaments
- use readable product interface screenshots as the main visual evidence
- use Indonesian language
- mobile-first
- highly accessible for adult institution administrators

Required homepage sections:
1. announcement bar
2. sticky navigation
3. hero with two conversion paths
4. product value statement
5. institution management problems
6. solution selector for School, Ma'had, TPQ, Masjid, and Foundation
7. product interface tour
8. free software explanation
9. recurring operational donation section
10. donation impact section
11. donation options
12. transparency and reporting
13. institution onboarding steps
14. service model
15. impact for institutions, teachers, students, parents, and jamaah
16. transparent product roadmap
17. FAQ
18. final dual CTA
19. comprehensive footer

Important content rules:
- Make “software gratis” and “jadi donatur tetap” prominent.
- Do not hide the donation message in the footer.
- Clearly explain that free software still has real operational costs.
- Do not imply that donations are mandatory for institutions.
- Do not use the term waqf unless governance and a registered nazhir are ready.
- Do not invent clients, users, metrics, testimonials, uptime, certifications,
  partners, or security claims.
- Clearly mark demo data and planned features.
- Do not claim that every listed module is already available.
- Use accurate status labels: available, in development, and planned.
- Use reusable Astro components and data structures.
- Keep most pages statically rendered.
- Use minimal client-side JavaScript.
- Add semantic HTML, accessibility, SEO metadata, Open Graph metadata,
  sitemap, robots.txt, and Organization and SoftwareApplication JSON-LD.
- Create forms for institution registration and recurring donor interest.
- Add loading, success, error, validation, and spam protection states.
- Apply the Hallmark build and critique process before finalizing.
- Target Lighthouse scores above 95.
```
